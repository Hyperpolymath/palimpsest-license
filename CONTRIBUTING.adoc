Thank you for your interest in contributing! Your expertise helps us build a more resilient future for creators. Here's how you can get involved.

== Code of Conduct

All contributors are expected to abide by our link:./CODE_OF_CONDUCT.adoc[Code of Conduct]. Please read it to understand the standards we uphold.

== Ways to Contribute

. **Propose Changes:** Have an idea for a new clause or an improvement? Open an issue using the "Proposal" template.
. **Report Issues:** Find a typo, a broken link, or an ambiguity? Open an issue using the "Bug Report" template.
. **Translate:** Help us make the licence globally accessible by contributing translations.
. **Write Documentation:** Improve our guides, FAQs, or create new examples.
. **Develop Tooling:** Help build compliance tools, validators, or integrations.

== Submitting a Pull Request

. Fork the repository.
. Create a new branch for your feature or fix: `git checkout -b feature/your-idea` or `fix/the-problem`.
. Make your changes. Ensure your code adheres to our style guides (see `.prettierrc.json` and `.eslintrc.json`).
. Commit your changes with a clear, descriptive message.
. Push your branch and open a Pull Request against the `main` branch.
. The Stewardship Council will review your PR.
